About Us

Construct A Vision was founded in July 2020 by Taylor Theriot and Chad Brewer, two builders with a passion for quality craftsmanship and a no-nonsense approach to getting the job done right. Based in Kingston, Oklahoma, they proudly serve communities across southern Oklahoma and North Texas, offering new construction, remodeling, and welding services. After seeing too many local builds done poorly, they set out to raise the bar — bringing honesty, skill, and pride back to the industry.
